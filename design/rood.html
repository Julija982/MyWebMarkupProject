Rood

Menu

At Rood you can expect refined dishes with a playful twist - French elegance meets Asian freshiness.

Start with light bites like salmon tartare, scallops, or pumpkin gyoza. For mains, choose from tender steak, slow-cooked pork belly, fresh sea bass, or spiced vegeterian eggplant. To share, there are oysters, artisan snacks, and sides.

End you evening with a sweet panna cotta, millefeulle, or cheese trolley. Feeling adventurous? Let the chef surprise you with a multi-course tasting menu.
