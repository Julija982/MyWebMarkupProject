BastiJan

Menu

BastiJan offers a richly varied à la carte menu with options for meat, fish, and vegetarian diners. Starters include things like pumpkin soup and rillettes, while main courses feature dishes such as swordfish, châteaubriand, and marinated tuna.

The desserts are well‐considered too; panna cotta and ice cream are among the treats mentioned. The wine list complements the food with both classic French and Italian bottles, alongside newer labels, allowing good pairing flexibility.
