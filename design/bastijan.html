BastiJan

Menu

BastiJan offers a richly varied à la carte menu with options for meat, fish, and vegetarian diners. Starters include fresh fig and pumpkin soup, and mains feature seasonal dishes such as swordfish, chickpea curry, and hand-rolled pasta.

Desserts are homemade — from panna cotta to ice cream — with new treats introduced regularly. The wine list complements the food with both classic French and Italian labels alongside newer vintages for perfect pairing.
