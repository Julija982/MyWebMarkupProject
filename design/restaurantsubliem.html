Restaurant Subliem

Menu

At Restaurant Subliem in Haarlem, the menu blends French-Mediterranean influences with a strong focus on fresh, organic, and sustainably sourced ingredients.

The starters strike a balance between refined classics like carpaccio or vitello tonnato and more playful vegetarian creations built around beets, goat cheese, or courgette soup with coconut milk.

Mains follow the same philosophy: heary dishes such as ribeye, calf's liver with apple and Calvados, or stuffed pork tenderioin sit comfortably alongside delicate fish like salmon or seabass, while vegetarian and vegan diners aren't left behind thanks to options like moussake or a vegetable Wellington.

Desserts lean indulgent but creative - from tiramisu and carrot cake to Middle - Eastern inspired pistachio and katalfi combinations and there's also a well - curated cheese board.
