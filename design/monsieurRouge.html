Monsieur Rouge

Menu

Monsieur Rouge aperitif dishes are available all day long. Think of a delicious pate, oven-baked Camembert, hand-cut steak tartare, or shrimp croquettes. These dishes can be enjoyed during lunch or with drinks. They can also be ordered as a starter.

At Monsieur Rouge, you can enjoy a delicious brunch or lunch. Whether its a romantic date, cozy get-together with friends, a business meeting, or family, you can enjoy the sun by the water with a glass of wine or a beer.

From brunch to aperitifs and on to dinner. From 17:00, Monsieur Rouge offers delicious main courses on the menu. Beef, burgers, fish or vegeterian. Their menu is small, but everything is fresh and lovingly prepared. Naturally, it comes with a matching glass of French wine.
